  • You may already be familiar with the idea of circuits with resistors in series or parallel or some combination of the two.

    • Series

The same current flows in each resistor, the voltages across them are typically different, where V = V1 + V2 + V3 which leads to the equivalent resistance formula

Req = R1 + R2 + R3
